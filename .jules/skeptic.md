## 2026-07-07 - Avoid Unnecessary Inheritance and Single-Use Abstractions\n**Learning:** Interfaces like `GraphElement` that only exist to share a few properties (`id`, `tags`, `attributes`) between a very small number of concrete types (like nodes and edges) without being used polymorphically introduce unnecessary cognitive load and "deep interface hierarchies."\n**Action:** Follow the Skeptic persona's directives to remove such single-use abstractions. Inline shared properties directly into the concrete types (`GraphNode`, `GraphEdge`) to keep the domain model flat, clear, and easy to read without bouncing between files.
